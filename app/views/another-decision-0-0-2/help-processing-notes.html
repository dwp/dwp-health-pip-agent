Recording decision notes

'My decision notes' will be at the top of all screens moving forward. This is for you to record your notes as you look at the decision again. You'll be able to cut and paste content within the service into the notes.

The notes will:

  • follow you throughout the whole decision reconsideration process
  • help you when making a call to the person
  • automatically save once a minute, or sooner if you need to

There will be an opportunity to tidy your notes at the end.
