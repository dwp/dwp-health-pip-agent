Accessing a persons record

To help you reference information within person record you can use the quicklink at the top of the page

The quick link is the persons name.

The person record will open in a new tab.

Person record will allow you to see information such as:

  • contact detials
  • delegated authorities
  • flags and markers
  • accesablity needs
