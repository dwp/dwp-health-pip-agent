Reconsider a decision

Before you start, make sure the person who has asked for their decision to be reconsidered understands:

  • that PIP is assessed on how their condition affects them, not the condition itself
  • it is to help with extra living costs if they have difficulty doing certain everyday tasks or getting around because of their condition
  • what their current decision means

Explain what will happen next

  • we'll record the details of which parts of the decision they disagree with
  • there'll be an opportunity to send additional evidence to support the application
  • someone will look at all the information again and make another decision
  • their points could go up or down
