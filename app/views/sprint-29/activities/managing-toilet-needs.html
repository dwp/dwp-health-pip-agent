Managing toilet needs or incontinence

Using the toilet means being able to get on or off a standard toilet, and cleaning yourself after using the toilet. Managing incontinence means emptying your bowel and bladder including if you need a collecting device such as a bottle, bucket or catheter, and cleaning yourself after doing do.
