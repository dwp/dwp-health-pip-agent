Dressing and undressing

Health information gather (PIP2)

Asked in application

Tell us about difficulties you have dressing and undressing, and how you manage them

Response in application

On a bad day, I struggle with shoes and other clothes. My clothes feel tight, particularly trousers. I wear PJs most of the time.

Assessment report findings

Some difficulty reported is consistent with his diagnosis of fibromyalgia.

He is prescribed medication for nerve pain and is known to the pain management team, though is currently just having phone calls with a clinical psychologist to manage his pain, he has nothing prescribed for more severe pain to suggest a greater restriction.

It is reasonable to suggest he requires aids or adapated clothing to complete this reliably and within a timely manner.

The need for assistance has been considered and ruled out, he has no medication prescribed for severe pain, no input from rheumatology.

He is still employed as a cleaner, working 12 hours per week over 5 days, supporting adequate function to use aids rather than assistance.
