Understand applicants ability when Preparing food

As asked in application

Does your condition affect you preparing food,
or prevent you from doing so?

Answer from the citizen

Yes

As asked in application

Tell us about the difficulties you have with
preparing food and how you manage them

Answer from the citizen

About twice a year, for about 3 weeks at a time I have difficulty remembering to turn the hob off because I get too distracted to complete tasks like this. My partner supervises me in the kitchen to make sure I stay safe.

Every day, I have difficulty standing while preparing food because my leg is too stiff and painful. I need to use a perching stool so I can rest my leg while preparing food.
