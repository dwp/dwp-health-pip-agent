Preparing food

This means making a simple, one course meal for one.

This includes:

  • to use an aid or appliance to be able to take nutrition; or
  • supervision to be able to take nutrition; or
  • assistance to be able to cut up food

Do consider if you can prepare food safely and without being supervised.
